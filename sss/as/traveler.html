Traveler

Core Details

Identity & Goal: Player chooses either Aether (male) or Lumine (female). The other sibling becomes leader of the Abyss Order.

Powers: Traveler is a "descender" not originally from Teyvat. Can switch elements and skilled with sword combat.

Gameplay & Role: Versatile 5-star character; can change elemental affinities at Statue of The Seven.

Personality & Lore: Silent protagonist at first, compassionate and curious. Ancient, existing through the birth and death of stars.

Key Aspects: Paimon as companion. Powerful combat abilities. Voiced by Zach Aguilar (English) and Shun Horie (Japanese).
