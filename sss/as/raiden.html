Raiden Shogun

Core Details

Identity & Lore: "Ei", Electro Archon of Inazuma. Created a puppet to govern while meditating. Initially cold, later a protector.

Weapon & Element: Polearm, Electro

Role: Main DPS / Sub DPS

Abilities: Musou no Hitotachi (Elemental Burst) deals AoE Electro damage and regenerates energy for party.

Team Role: Highly versatile, fits into multiple team setups (e.g., "Raiden National").

Key Story Elements: Vision Hunt Decree, Puppet "Scaramouche", Transformation after character quest. Voiced by Anne Yatco (English).
