Character R: Raiden Shogun

Overview & Lore

Identity Raiden Shogun, also known as Raiden Ei or Beelzebul, is the Electro Archon and ruler of Inazuma in Genshin Impact. She embodies the ideal of "eternity" and enforces it through her governance. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
Archon & Puppet Ei created the Shogun puppet to govern Inazuma while she meditates in the Plane of Euthymia. The puppet carries out her will but is separate in consciousness. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
Personality Stoic and authoritarian at first, Raiden Shogun pursues eternal stability for her nation; after significant events (e.g., the Vision Hunt Decree), she reevaluates her approach. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
Voice Actors English: Anne Yatco; Chinese: Juhuahua; Japanese: Miyuki Sawashiro; Korean: Park Ji-yoon. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Gameplay & Role

Element & Weapon Electro; Polearm (commonly), switching to sword (Musou Isshin) during Elemental Burst. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
Role Playable 5★ character; excels as DPS or support with energy recharge and burst damage mechanics. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
Signature Skills Elemental Skill grants the “Eye of Stormy Judgment,” boosting allies’ energy and damage; Elemental Burst unleashes powerful Electro AoE attacks using Resolve stacks. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

  • Sakoku & Vision Hunt Decrees: Policies enacted to isolate Inazuma and seize Visions in pursuit of eternity, shaping her story arc. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
  • Growth: Through conflict and reflection, Ei loosens her strict pursuit of eternal stability and opens her nation. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
