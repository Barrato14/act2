Character A: The Traveler

Overview & Lore

Identity & Goal The player chooses either Aether (male) or Lumine (female). The other sibling becomes the leader of the Abyss Order.
Powers A "descender" not originally from Teyvat, capable of using elements directly. Skilled in sword combat and possesses immense, initially sealed power.
Gameplay & Role Versatile 5-star character who can switch elemental affinities by interacting with Statues of the Seven across Teyvat.
Story & Personality Initially a silent protagonist, now more expressive. Compassionate, often initially reluctant to intervene in local politics, acting as an outsider searching for their sibling.
Lore Ancient being, existing through the birth and death of stars. Known by an "Ancient Name" (TMN) in Natlan.

Key Aspects

  • Paimon: Essential guide and travel companion.
  • Combat: Lore-wise extremely powerful and adaptable; jack-of-all-trades in gameplay.
  • Voice Actors: Aether: Zach Aguilar (English), Shun Horie (Japanese).
  • The Traveler's journey is ongoing, playing a central role in the overarching mysteries of Teyvat.
