Fantasy RPG Info

This website draws inspiration from popular action RPGs, especially Genshin Impact. It features:

  • Character design layouts with elemental roles (e.g., Electro, Hydro, Pyro, Anemo).
  • Clean table-based organization for stats, lore, and abilities.
  • Immersive thematic backgrounds for a fantasy aesthetic.
  • Focus on storytelling and world-building, similar to Teyvat's regions.
  • Expandable design to eventually include talents, weapons, and constellation mechanics.

While not affiliated with miHoYo, this site is a homage to the style and structure of games like Genshin Impact, demonstrating open-world fantasy elements and RPG mechanics in a simple, web-friendly format.
